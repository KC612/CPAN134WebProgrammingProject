THE JOURNEY OF A LIFETIME

Our contemporary Toronto hotel features a variety of flexible function spaces, many of which are illuminated by large windows letting in natural light. Located in the heart of Toronto’s Yorkville neighbourhood, just minutes from the business district and designer shopping, it is the perfect venue for your next conference or special occasion.

LIGHT AND AIRY VENUES

Whether you’re hosting a conference, a product launch or a board meeting, our conveniently located hotel can accommodate your needs with personalized service. Ballrooms are adjacent to breakout rooms for easy conferences, or sponsor a more casual business meeting or VIP reception in a guest suite.

GOURMET GROUP ACTIVITIES

Treat members of your group to a wine tasting on-site or at nearby Niagara-area wineries. Introduce them to the art of making nitrogen ice cream. Fly them to Lake Muskoka for a gourmet luncheon in the wilderness. The options for food-themed group activities are endless.

INTIMATE OCCASIONS

Our French brasserie, Café Boulud, offers semi-private dining for small groups up to 10 people. Diners can select their own appetizer, entrée and dessert from a prix-fixe menu.

FAMILY PERKS

Families staying with children under 18 years of age will receive one complimentary in-room movie and a family popcorn amenity, a backpack and slippers, and use of our board games, Yoto Player and a PlayStation 4 or Nintendo Switch set up in their room (on request and subject to availability).
