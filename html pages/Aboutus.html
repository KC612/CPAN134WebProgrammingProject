About Us

Welcome to BigFin, Toronto's newest surf and turf sensation!
Nestled in the heart of the city, our restaurant offers a delightful blend of aquatic delights and savory land-based dishes that are sure to make waves in your taste buds. Whether you're a seafood aficionado or a land lover, there's something for every palate to savor at BinFin. So come on in and let us sea you soon!


Our Mission

Our goal is to create a welcoming space where people of all ages can come together to savor delicious food. While our restaurant operates as an all-ages venue for the most part, we also offer exclusive member events and bookings for those aged 19 and above, in line with legal drinking regulations. However, we haven't forgotten about our younger guests – family-friendly bookings, such as kids' birthday events, are also available, ensuring that everyone feels included in our community. By nurturing a strong community, we can create a space where friendships flourish, memories are made, and every visit feels like coming home.

Our Story

After graduating from Humber College, Osasere Obazogbon, Katelynn Caceres, and Joseph Waldron, three passionate computer programming students, embarked on this culinary journey together. With their innovative spirit and commitment to excellence, they've transformed their dream into a reality, inviting guests to indulge in flavorful dishes while supporting an enviornmental cause they believe in.
