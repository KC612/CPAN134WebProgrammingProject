Line Cook

We are looking for an experienced line cook to join our kitchen team.

  • Prepares ingredients for menu items
  • Cooks food items according to recipe specifications
  • Ensures food safety and sanitation standards are met
  • Collaborates with kitchen staff to maintain a clean and organized work environment

Head Chef

We are looking for an experienced Head Chef to lead the kitchen team.

  • Develops and creates new menu items
  • Manages kitchen staff and schedules
  • Oversees food preparation and presentation
  • Ensures quality and consistency in all dishes
  • Orders and maintains inventory of kitchen supplies

Sous Chef

Join our team as a Sous Chef and assist our Head Chef in the preparation of delicious seafood dishes.

  • Prepares seafood and other ingredients for cooking
  • Assists in developing new recipes and menu items
  • Supervises kitchen staff in the absence of the Head Chef

Server

We are looking for a friendly serving staff who is great at interacting with customers.

  • Provides excellent customer service to guests
  • Takes food and beverage orders accurately
  • Delivers food and drinks to tables in a timely manner
  • Assists with cleaning and organizing dining areas
  • Handles guest inquiries and resolves complaints professionally
